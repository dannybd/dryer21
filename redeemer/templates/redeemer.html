Redeeming a Dryer 21 Bond



What is this?

We provide a Tor hidden service for anonymizing bitcoin transactions while preventing even our own server from linking from and to addresses. We accomplish this through the use of "bitcoin bonds", generated through a process known as blind signing. This allows a users to purchase and redeem bonds, without us (the server) being able to link sold and redeemed bonds. Such a setup has allows Alice to send Bob a bond's worth of bitcoin, without:

  • Alice learning Bob's bitcoin address,
  • Bob learning Alice's bitcoin address, or
  • our server being able to link Alice's bond purchase with Bob's bond redemption.
